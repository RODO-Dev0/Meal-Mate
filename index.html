Privacy Policy & Terms
Last updated: 2025
1. Data Storage

MealMate stores all your data — saved recipes, meal plans, grocery lists, and preferences — exclusively on your own device using your browser's localStorage. No data is ever transmitted to any server controlled by MealMate.

2. AI Features

When you use the AI suggestion feature, your selected ingredient names are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate meal ideas. No personal identifiers are included. Please review Google's privacy policy at ai.google.dev for details on how they handle API requests.

3. No Accounts Required

MealMate requires no registration, login, or personal information of any kind. We do not collect your name, email, location, or any personal data.

4. No Analytics or Tracking

MealMate contains no tracking scripts, analytics, advertising, or third-party cookies. Your usage is completely private.

5. Halal Compliance

While we strive to ensure all suggestions are halal, users are responsible for verifying ingredients and recipes comply with their personal requirements. MealMate provides information only and does not constitute religious certification.

6. Terms of Use

MealMate is provided as-is for personal, non-commercial use. The recipe database is for guidance only. Always ensure food is cooked safely and to appropriate temperatures. MealMate is not liable for any outcomes from use of recipe suggestions.

7. Contact

For any questions about this policy, please contact us through the app store listing.
